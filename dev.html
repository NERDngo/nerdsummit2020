HOW DOES THIS VIRTUAL SUMMIT WORK?

Virtual NERD Summit is like the in-person summit, except that session rooms are virtual (a Zoom session) rather than physical.

  • Find the session you wish to attend in the schedule below.
  • Click the JOIN * link ~5 minutes (or less) before the session is scheduled to start (or see room links below).
    (If the host is not there, he/she should be there shortly)
    * the JOIN links do not exist yet, we will add them later.
  • The sessions are recorded. Mute your audio and/or turn off your video if you do not want to be in the recording.
  • Please stay muted unless you are asking a question.
  • To ask a question, post "I have a question" in the Zoom chat, and you will be called on.
  • When the talk is over, the Zoom session will close.

Then repeat the above for the next session that you would like to attend.

If you do not already have it, download the Zoom Client for Meetings *
* You will be prompted to do so when you click a JOIN link if you do not have it, but it is better to do it in advance.

NERDS2020 Hackathon on Sunday!

NERDS2020 Hackathon will take place on Sunday, March 22nd from 9am to 4pm EST and will be located at the Integrated Sciences Building at UMass Amherst

Do you like to code? Do you want to code? Have you tried to learn to code only to not know how to get started?

Using JavaScript, Python, and maybe a Raspberry Pi or two, beginner programmers and professionals (ages 8 and up) will have opportunities to learn practical programming skills and participate in building real-world applications with mentors in a group setting.

Come join us for a day of networking, learning, and practical hacking -- make new friends, sharpen your skills and develop new ones in a fun and encouraging setting. Sign-up is here.

About

New England Regional Developers

NERD Summit is a product of New England Regional Developers (NERD). NERD creates positive and supportive learning opportunities for people who work, or want to work in web and related technologies in the New England area.
